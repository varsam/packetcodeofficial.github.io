OUR AIM

The main aim of this “Gopal Krishna Gaushla & camp; Rajvidyasadan” is the conservation and maintenance of the cow breed of “Brij”. “Shri Brij Chaurasi”, the playfield of Lord Shri Krishna, which used to have cow breed in crores, has in comparison been reduced to just 2 to 3 lakhs. The scale at which the cow race (Gau-Vansh) is being slaughtered and disregarded, that day is not far away when the cow race would be a rare sight. The importance of the honorable mother cow and moreover even in this, the importance of the cow of “Brij” area cannot be explained in words. The saints are devoted to provide complete protection to the whole homeless cow race (Gau-Vansh) of the “Brij” area in the “Surabhi Van”. The cows have started to come in this Surbhi Van. Presently there are about 1500 cow race in the cow shed. The point to note is that the cow race (Gau-Vansh) which comes in a sickly state in the cow favorable environment of this nature friendly place starts to appear healthy in just 1 month for the dwelling of Mother cows, 28 Ft wide and 2000 Ft long house, 8000 Ft Chaff Houses, Huts for Saints, Cattleman Kitchen (Gwaal Rasoi), a couple of two roomed cottages for the “Vaanprasthis” and 50 Ft water tank of 3 lakh Litre has been constructed. Apart from this, 3200Ft Pantry, Residential Rooms and Plantation work is going on with great speed.

Thousands of cow shelters, chaff stores, pantries, cattleman houses and saints huts have to be constructed and good deal of plots have to be bought in order to render speed to the sacred work of the cow conservation and maintenance in accordance with the extreme commitment of the great saints. We call forth all the generous cow lover devotees to come forward and cooperate with their body, heart and money. Free ranging of cows is the main aim of this place. The saints are devoted towards the free ranging of the cow race (Gau-vansh) in this area, on the mountains and in the forests.

 

The donation of land for mother cow is the everlasting donation (Akshay Daan). In the holy tradition of the eternal Hindu culture, the land donation for the ranging of mother cow is considered very valuable. It is called “Akshay Punya” in the Vedic Academics (Ved Shaastra), that does not complete, that does not ruin or decay, that is called “Akshay”. The goddess earth, in its substantive form is indestructible. This is why the land never ruins, never completes, never decays. Therefore, land donation is essentially called “Akshay Daan”.

In the end, we again request all our religious and dutiful brothers and sisters that everybody with their whole strength and capacity must avail the virtuous benefit of donating the land for cow ranging. This is an opportunity! Buy more and more land and dedicate it to the cows on your behalf submitting it to the cow serving organizations and cow serving commissions. Whereby, Cow serving campaign by becoming progressive can successfully execute the universal welfare work of conservation, husbandry and maintenance of cow race (Gau-Vansh). Today, the biggest hindrance in cow service is the scarcity of ranging land. Cow Service is difficult and impossible without ranging land. Therefore the religion loving public is expected to donate land as a sacrificial offering in the cow service sacrifice.
